Pictures Survey

In this study, you will be asked to consider whether different kinds of things (e.g., a dog, a computer) have certain kinds of abilities or experiences.

The study should take about 4-6 minutes to complete. Please complete the study in one sitting, without interruption. At the end, you'll be able to see your own results.
